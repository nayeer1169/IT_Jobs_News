## Global Situation

The tech sector continues its structural transition. Rather than simple post-pandemic adjustments, companies are executing deep organizational changes to fund massive computational hardware layouts. Amdocs and Oracle's reductions highlight how enterprises are automating software maintenance, database support, and operations. Non-technical corporate divisions, such as middle management, operations, finance, and legal support, are experiencing higher redundancy rates as companies deploy automated solutions to reduce bureaucratic lag.

**Roles at higher risk:**
- Middle management and corporate administration
- Operational finance and legal support teams
- Manual QA testing and generalist coding roles
- Non-AI-integrated technical roles

**Roles less exposed:**
- AI/ML researchers and deep learning practitioners
- Cloud infrastructure architects and SREs
- Cybersecurity specialists and network architects
- Core data platform engineers maintaining ETL pipelines

## India Situation

Indian technical talent continues to adjust to conservative hiring among major IT services providers. While entry-level opportunities are constrained, Global Capability Centers (GCCs) are maintaining active, selective recruitment, particularly for teams handling cybersecurity and data platform migrations.
