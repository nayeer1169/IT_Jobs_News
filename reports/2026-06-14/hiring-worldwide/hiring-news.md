## Skills In Demand

Recruiters are passing over traditional resume markers in favor of deep specialized skills:

- Artificial Intelligence (AI) and Machine Learning (ML) engineering
- Advanced Cloud Architecture (GCP, AWS, Azure DevOps pipelines)
- Enterprise Data Engineering (ETL pipelines, data lake management)
- Defensive Cybersecurity and threat monitoring
- Automated testing and QA frameworks (Selenium, Cypress, Playwright)

## Fresher Action Plan For Today

1. Emphasize "Skills-First" proof: Highlight personal, verifiable projects on GitHub showcasing end-to-end integration of APIs or cloud deployment.
2. Focus on Tier-2/3 GCC options: Look for newer office expansions in cities outside the major metros, where competition is slightly lower.
3. Master AI collaboration: Be ready to show how you use AI developer tools to write, debug, and optimize code quickly during technical assessments.
4. Prepare for coding tests: Ensure you have solid practice in basic DSA, DBMS, and OS concepts, as screening standards are increasingly selective.
