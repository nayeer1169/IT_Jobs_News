## Global Situation

The tech industry is increasingly employing a combination of active layoffs and "ghost downsizing" (choosing not to backfill vacated positions). As part of their AI strategy, large companies like Wix and Oracle are scaling down customer support, marketing, and mid-level coordination layers to divert cash flow to graphics hardware, server leases, and specialized AI/ML engineering roles.

**Roles at higher risk:**
- Non-automated customer support and operations roles
- Non-AI-integrated general software developers
- Marketing and mid-level administrative support
- QA manual testers and legacy system consultants

**Roles less exposed:**
- Machine Learning (MLOps) and deep learning engineers
- Site Reliability Engineers (SREs) and cloud architects
- Cybersecurity operations and threat analysts
- Core data platform developers managing pipelines

## India Situation

Indian IT services hubs are experiencing muted recruitment volumes. However, Global Capability Centers (GCCs) are maintaining a stable presence, insourcing high-value software projects. Many developers are upskilling in LLM fine-tuning and cloud architecture to align with GCC hiring profiles.
