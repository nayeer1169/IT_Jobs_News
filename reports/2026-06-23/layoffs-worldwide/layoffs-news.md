## Global Situation

The tech sector is currently in a state of "rebalance" rather than a broad cyclical recession. Companies are under intense pressure to invest in AI infrastructure, causing them to downsize legacy product teams, administration, and customer service. Oracle's massive contraction of 21,000 roles highlights how major enterprises are using generative AI to automate database management, consulting, and customer operations.

**Roles at higher risk:**
- Legacy software developers and generalist coding roles
- Customer operations, support, and consulting positions
- Non-automated QA testing and validation roles
- Middle management and operational coordination layers

**Roles less exposed:**
- Machine Learning (ML) engineers and AI developers
- Cloud security engineers and SREs
- Data infrastructure and ETL pipeline engineers
- Government and defense technology roles requiring security clearances

## India Situation

In India, developers face severe competition due to traditional IT service companies maintaining near-zero net headcount growth. However, Global Capability Centers (GCCs) remain the most resilient employers, offering opportunities to professionals skilled in cloud architecture and data engineering.
