## Global Situation

The tech sector continues its structural "AI realignment." Major tech companies are seeking to optimize headcount in customer support and administrative functions. By automating customer facing workflows, companies are aiming to offset cost pressures and redeploy investments into AI computing capacity. Furthermore, the gaming industry is experiencing a severe contraction, with over 3,700 positions cut YTD.

**Roles at higher risk:**
- Customer support and success representatives
- Game developers, QA testers, and designers in legacy studios
- Middle management and administrative support personnel
- Generalist software engineers

**Roles less exposed:**
- Machine Learning operations (MLOps) engineers
- Data engineers maintaining core data platforms
- Cybersecurity infrastructure engineers
- Cloud cost optimization specialists

## India Situation

Indian IT developers are seeing increased domestic demand from Global Capability Centers (GCCs), which are insourcing more advanced projects. However, entry-level opportunities remain highly restricted. Developers are encouraged to focus on system architecture and cloud security skills to improve employability.
